Angular Lifecycle hooks
Methods that are called at different points of a components creation/execution/destruction

Purely for purposes of fine tuning properties at different points

ngOnChanges() - executed anytime a change is detected on a data binding
ngOnInit() - occurs when component is initializing
ngDoCheck() - trigger upon ngOnChanges() being triggered as well as any methods
that may change the page or attributes
ngAfterContentInit() - after content is created for view
ngAfterContentChecked - after content is checked
ngAfterViewInit() - after view gets initialized for user
ngAfterViewChecked() - 
ngOnDestroy() - same as java destroy
